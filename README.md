### 🚚 Learning-Augmented Last-Mile Routing

An interpretable hierarchical zone-preference model trained on **6,112 Amazon delivery routes**.

**Results**
- **39.9%** lower aggregate median official route score
- **88.1%** of unseen routes improved
- Median zone re-entries reduced from **21 to 0**
- Validation-only model selection and route-level robustness analysis

**Stack**

`Python` `Operations Research` `Interpretable ML` `Route Optimization`

### 📦 Dispatch Delays & Late-Delivery Risk

A quasi-causal supply-chain study using **81,941 Olist orders** and five-fold cross-fitted AIPW.

**Results**
- Estimated **14.11 percentage-point** adjusted risk increase
- 95% CI: **12.81–15.41 pp**
- Maximum weighted |SMD| of **0.053**
- Placebo, temporal, cohort and nonlinear robustness checks

**Stack**

`Python` `Causal Inference` `AIPW` `Supply Chain Analytics`
